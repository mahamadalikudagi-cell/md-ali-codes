Chandrayaan Missions Timeline

India’s Journey to the Moon by ISRO

Chandrayaan-1 (2003–2008)
Launch: 22 Oct 2008

Planning & Development: India’s first lunar mission, international collaboration.

  • First Indian lunar mission
  • Discovered water molecules on the Moon
  • High-resolution lunar maps
Chandrayaan-2 (2008–2019)
Launch: 22 July 2019

Planning & Development: Orbiter + Lander + Rover; soft landing challenges.

  • Orbiter operational; studies lunar surface & water
  • Lander lost during landing attempt
  • Enhanced ISRO’s landing expertise
Chandrayaan-3 (2016–2023)
Launch: 14 July 2023

Planning & Development: Focused on Lander + Rover; improved landing systems.

  • Successful soft landing near lunar south pole
  • Rover exploring soil, minerals, radiation
  • Proved India’s precision landing capability
